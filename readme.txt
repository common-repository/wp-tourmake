WP Tourmake generates shortcodes that allow you to quickly and easily add your Tourmake's and Viewmake's virtual tours to your website pages.

== Description ==

WP Tourmake gives you a new chance to promote your business by adding to your website your Tourmake's and Viewmake's virtual tours. You just have to copy and paste our shortcodes on your pages.

= Try WP Tourmake Pro. =
[WP Tourmake Pro](https://wp.tourmake.it/wp-tourmake-pro/) gives you the opportunity to enrich your website's pages with Tourmake hotspots and to create a gallery with your Viewmakes.
The display of the hotspots is fully customizable according to the style that best suits your website and it's responsive, to adapt to mobile devices like tablets and smartphones.
WP Tourmake Pro gives you the option to enable/disable the display of the hotspots related to your virtual tour: in this way you will be able to better illustrate to visitors the products and services you offer!

== Features ==

*Tourmake shortcode
*Viewmake shortcode

**Pro version adds**
* Tourmake hotspots shortcode
* Viewmake gallery shortcode
* Syncronization with your Viewmake account

== Important ==
The plugin uses Tourmake API to get data from Tourmake. All the received data is cached in WordPress database. You can delete or update cached data. When you paste your Tourmake shortcode on your website page, Tourmake will load some JS and embedded tour content. Tourmake embeds are regulated under terms of [Tourmake privacy policy](http://tourmake.it/it/privacy_policy).

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. You can start generating shortcodes with WP Tourmake.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What is Tourmake? =

Tourmake amplifies the potentialities of Google Street View by providing to Google trusted photographers a new interface with innovative and customizable elements for booking services. You can find more information on [Tourmake](https://www.tourmake.it/en/)

= Does it work with any theme? =

Sure! Our plugin will work with any theme, but may require some styling to make it match nicely.

= What does WP Tourmake plugin do?

**WP Tourmake** is a completely customizable, responsive solution to help you display your Tourmake's and Viewmake's virtual tours on your WordPress website.
Add as many tours as you want to your website pages using shortcodes.

= How can I create shortcodes with this plugin?

To configure a new shortcode, navigate to **WP Tourmake > My tours** page and click on **Add new**.
Fill the form with tour informations and customizations and save settings to create the shortcode.
